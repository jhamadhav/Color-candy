STORY :

You have been chosen by the benevolent king of the candy land to protect it by surviving the fall of candy loops from the sky.
These loops are a threat to all the fluffy (i.e people of candy land),you are the only one powerful enough to pass through all FORTY of them and save our precious candy land.

BE VICTORIOUS HERO !

INSTRUCTION :

1. Use touch in case for handheld device and cursor for desktop/laptop to control the player.

2. Collect 40 points in each difficulty level to finish the game.

3.Select difficulty level and other feature in the setting option.

4.Use the fullscreen button on top right corner to make the game cover all your screen.
FULLSCREEN INCREASES THE FUN BY DOUBLE !!
